About

Hello there! I'm Subarna, a Frontend web developer based near Sydney. I believe in starting my day by tackling the challenging tasks head-on, setting the tone for a productive day ahead. What gets me out of bed every morning? The excitement of bringing ideas to life on the vast canvas of the web.

People who know me say I'm a bit obsessed, and honestly, they're not wrong. I'm fueled by a relentless drive and an insatiable curiosity to learn new things.

Recently, I set a personal challenge to read a book every day, exposing myself to diverse fields and ideas. If you're someone who shares a passion for continuous learning and building things that make a difference, I'd love to connect. Let's chat!
